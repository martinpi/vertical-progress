Globetrotters

 
 

Ablate planets into extinction in the ever lasting race for space crystals!

Globetrotters is a game for up to 4 players. Each player uses a single button to boost from planet to planet with their jetpack equipped astronaut. Collect all the crystals before any of the other players can get them! Running on the surface of a planet will slowly shrink it until it's gone. After 5 rounds, the player with the most crystals wins the game.

Globetrotters is a game by Broken Rules that was developed for the GAMMA IV one button game design challenge. It is free for you to enjoy. Now and forever (or until your iOS version is too new to run it)!
