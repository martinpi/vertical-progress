VERTICAL PROGRESS

Hi there. I am Martin Pichlmair, Associate Professor and Head of the Games Programme at IT University Copenhagen. I am also one out of five co-founders of award winning boutique game studio Broken Rules and a member of the Copenhagen Game Collective. I research game design, create tools and processes for game design and development, and regularly cook up fresh prototypes. And I love dangerously hot food.

What can I do for you? I regularly speak at industry and academic conferences, I consult in game design for indie games as well as playful design/gamification projects.
