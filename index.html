VERTICAL PROGRESS

Hi there. I am Martin Pichlmair, and I work with the playful side of technology. Currently I'm most interested in Creative AI and setting up a startup in the AI writing space called Write with LAIKA.

Interactive toys I've created are sometimes games, often take the shape of tools, and other times turn out to be weird experiments.

I work as an Associate Professor and Co-Head of the Games Programme at IT University Copenhagen. I am also one out of five co-founders of award winning boutique game studio Broken Rules and a member of the Copenhagen Game Collective.

I love dangerously hot food.
